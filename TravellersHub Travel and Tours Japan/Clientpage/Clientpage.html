Osaka
Osaka is approximately 2.5hrs away from Tokyo by Shinkansen. It offers plenty of fun activities & attractions, shopping, and nightlife, and is also a great place to discover Japanese heritage. Osaka is also the best travel destination if you want to try affordable and delicious Japanese food like Takoyaki, Okonomiyaki, ramen and so much more!.

Sapporo
Sapporo is the capital and largest city of the northern island of Hokkaido. One of Japan's newest and most orderly cities, it has little in the way of traditional architecture.
