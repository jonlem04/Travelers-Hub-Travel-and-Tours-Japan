JAPAN VISA

What you need to know

Planning your trip to Japan but getting overwhelmed with how to process your visa? With UHI, Japan visa processing is made easy and hassle-free! Our team of dedicated documentation professionals will help you in every step of the process from email consultation, online application, and preparation of requirements, up to tracking of your visa status.

PURPOSE

    Applicant himself/herself made the arrangements for the travel/tour in Japan. If applicant request a multiple-entry visa for short-term stay, please refer to the requirement for Multiple Entry Visa for Tourism.

REQUIREMENTS (All documents issued/prepared in the Philippines must be original except those specifically stated as photocopy.)

  • Broken passports are not accepted. Passports must be signed and have at least two (2) blank visa pages.

REMINDERS

  • 1. Size of document for application should be A4 size only. If document is other size, please submit copy that is already adjusted in A4 size from its original and without staples or pasting pages.
  • 2. Applicants are exempted from submitting (4) and (5) indicated above if they can submit old/valid passports with used Japan visa. For family application with Guarantor, applicant is still required to submit item (4) or (5) whichever is applicable, even with previous Japan Visa to prove relationship.
  • 3. A photocopy of a used Japanese visa is not honored as a replacement for a physical passport with a used Japanese visa.
  • 4. It is applicant’s responsibility to ensure that he/she meet the requirements for the grant of a visa. Submission of any supporting documents not listed above is encouraged (e.g. applicant’s economic or social ties with the Philippines, urgent reasons for visit: medical certificate, wedding invitation).
  • 5. Submission of requirements does not guarantee issuance of visa.
  • 6. If document submitted for application is verified by Embassy or Consular Office as fake or tampered, visa is not issued.
  • 7. Documents submitted for application will not be returned.
  • 8. Documents that will not comply with the requirements of the embassy maybe terminated.
  • 9. Applicants are given three (3) months to submit additional requirements. Otherwise, the application will be terminated.
  • 10. Reason for the denial of visa will not be disclosed even if an inquiry is made. At the same time, if a visa application is denied, applicant may only re-apply for the same purpose after (6) months.
